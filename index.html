Rules :

  • It is a 2 player game.
  • Each post can stack 3 dots.
  • The game starts after each player gets one post.
  • Post explodes after stacking 3 dots.
  • After the game starts if there is no post of one color, the other player wins.
  • After exploding, it takes nearby posts as well. The nearby posts may also explode if 3 dots are already present causing a chain reaction.
